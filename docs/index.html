Welcome to Waggle Dance!


From Gfycat; a bee's waggle dance.

Waggle Dance is a website devoted to classifying bees and giving ways to help. Waggle Dance is also my project for my Girl Scout Gold Award. My Gold Award is about using technology to bring awareness about bees and their diminishing population. Visit our classifier to find out what kind of bees are in your backyard.
